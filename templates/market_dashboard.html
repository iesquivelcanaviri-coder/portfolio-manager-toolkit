Market Dashboard

What this dashboard does

This page helps a portfolio manager screen securities before moving to detailed analysis. It is designed to reduce the list quickly by applying return, volatility, ranking, and conviction filters.

What each smart preset means

Low Risk: highlights more stable securities with lower market sensitivity. Typically includes assets with beta at or below 1 and moderate volatility. These are commonly used for capital preservation, income generation, or defensive positioning within a portfolio.

High Conviction (6% position): identifies the strongest investment ideas based on expected return, volatility, and risk-adjusted performance. These are high-confidence opportunities where the manager allocates more capital.

Core Holdings (3.5% position): represents stable, well-balanced investments that form the foundation of the portfolio.

Satellite / Diversifiers (3% position): includes supporting positions used to enhance diversification or gain exposure to specific sectors.

Exploratory / High Risk (1–2% position): captures higher-risk opportunities with smaller allocations to control downside risk.

Income / Defensive: focuses on securities providing stability or income such as bonds and defensive sectors.

Best Ranked: highlights securities with strong scores and acceptable risk-adjusted returns.

Cash (5% allocation): represents holding cash when no suitable investments are found or to manage risk.
